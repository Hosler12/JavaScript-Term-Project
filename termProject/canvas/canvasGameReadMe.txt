The canvas game is the third wheel of this unicycle. 

In this game, you attempt to get as far as you can by dodging the bars that come your way.

You dodge bars by clicking the movement buttons below the game, and you get more points the longer you survive.

The game will record your high score within local storage so you can attempt to reach new heights.
